I've been working in e-commerce and digital marketing since 2016 - when I launched my first e-shop. Since then I've helped build e-commerce and SaaS projects across Slovakia, Czech Republic, and Europe.

From 2019 to 2022 I led marketing at Filmzie - a movie streaming platform startup. Between 2023 and 2025 I led the Slovak marketing team at Shoptet - one of Central Europe's leading e-commerce platforms.

Today I'm focused on three things: building my own supplement brand FeelAfterglow, helping grow the E-commerce Day private club for Slovak e-shop owners, and building marketing foundations for Polystate.io - a platform for sovereign individuals and digital nomads.

In 2026, I started building e-shops using AI tools like Claude. What used to take an agency months and thousands of euros, I can now deliver in days - without cutting corners on quality or brand.
