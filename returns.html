EU Consumer Rights

Under EU Directive 2011/83/EU, consumers have the right to withdraw from a distance contract within 14 days without giving any reason - unless the digital service has already begun with the consumer's prior express consent and acknowledgement that the right of withdrawal is lost upon commencement.

By purchasing and scheduling your kickoff session, you expressly consent to the immediate start of the service and acknowledge that the right of withdrawal may be lost once the service commences.
